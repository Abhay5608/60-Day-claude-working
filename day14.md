Today’s challenge focused on building and testing an AI Job Red Flag Detector using Claude AI to evaluate job opportunities before applying.
Objective
Analyze a Job Description (JD) and Company Information to identify potential risks, workplace concerns, and hiring red flags that job seekers often miss.
What I Completed
✅ Gathered a Job Description and Company Information
✅ Used Claude AI with the Job Red Flag Detector prompt
✅ Generated a comprehensive Risk Analysis Report
✅ Evaluated the Overall Risk Score
✅ Identified Red Flags and Positive Signals
✅ Analyzed the Risk Breakdown
✅ Reviewed the Final Hiring Verdict
✅ Generated potential Interview Questions
✅ Documented findings and uploaded project assets to GitHub
Key Insights
Not every attractive job posting is a good opportunity.
AI can quickly identify unrealistic expectations and hidden workplace risks.
Company research is just as important as resume preparation.
Risk analysis helps candidates make informed career decisions.
Structured prompts can turn AI into a powerful career evaluation assistant.
