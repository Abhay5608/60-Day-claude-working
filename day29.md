GitHub Repository: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

📌 Description

An AI-powered Supply Chain Crisis Lab built with React that simulates real-world enterprise supply chain disruptions. Users analyze a randomly generated company, respond to crisis scenarios, negotiate with suppliers, make executive decisions, invest in AI technologies, and review business outcomes through an executive dashboard.

🚚 AI Supply Chain Crisis Lab

Overview

Supply Chain Crisis Lab is an interactive React-based business simulation that helps users understand enterprise supply chain management through realistic crisis scenarios.

Every simulation generates a unique company profile, crisis event, financial impact, supplier network, and business outcome.
